Welcome to our Island of Castaways Player.

We Need Your Help...

Monsters have overtaken the land and keep dumping their horrible waste into our waters, killing our sea. The only way to stop their pollution is by defeating them in a game. If you win, you stop that Monster from dumping their waste. If you lose, they release it all.

The Game:
The Monster chooses a random word. Then you guess Letter by Letter. You have a limited amount of incorrect guesses before you have lost the round. Each round lost,deducts One life Point. At 0 lives, The Monsters take over the island for good. But guess the word before running out of guesses, and you win the round. Go forth and defeat the Monsters!
Easy Mode: 10 Incorrect Guesses
Medium Mode: 7 Incorrect Guesses
Hard Mode: 5 Incorrect Guesses
High Score is calculated by total Points x Remaining Life Points
